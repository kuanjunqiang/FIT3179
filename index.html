The Choropleth Map

Choropleth map can show quantitative data perfectly, the greater luminance the value, the darker the colour. My choropleth map describes the total death cases of COVID-19 in year 2020 and the range of death cases is 10 to 10000, the greater the count of death cases, the darker the colour on the map.

The Heatmap

The heatmap shows the COVID-19 death rate based on different categories of ages. Different categories of ages have different probability to get infection and death rates. However, due to the evolutionary of COVID-19 virus, the bin which has darkest colour in the visualisation has the highest death rate within every categories of ages.

The Heatmap II

It clearly shows the daily new death of COVID-19 from January to July 2020. Obviously, the COVID-19 cases is become more and more serious especially from end of March. We can see the trend of virus spread along time and this is putting us in danger if we still don't take it serious.

The Scatter Plot

It shows the total COVID-19 tests in continents over total cases. The main purpose of this visualisation is to show that not everyone is tested on COVID-19 because the total cases confirmed is always higher than the amount of test. People who carry COVID-19 virus may be no effection to himself but he spreads the virus unintentionally daily.

The Interactive Multi-View Displays

GDP per capacita, the gross domestic product per capita, is a measure of a country's economic output that accounts for its number of people. It divides the country's gross domestic product by its total population. The visualisation shows the GDP per capacita over total deaths from January to July. The higher the GDP per capacita, the lower the death rate. Besides, the lower the GDP per capacita, the higher the death rate.
